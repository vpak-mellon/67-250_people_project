About

StudentStorage was founded in 2026 as a student-driven initiative to address the seasonal challenges of campus move-outs and short-term storage gaps. Developed in response to the lack of affordable and flexible options available to students, the platform brings together peers across campuses to better utilize existing space. By building on shared student experiences, StudentStorage aims to create a more coordinated and accessible approach to summer storage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

For Renters:

What makes StudentStorage different from traditional storage units?

We don’t own any storage units, but rather we connect students who need storage to students that have storage. This platform is a free market and students who are renting out their space can price however they like, capped for different sizing options.

This system is designed to serve multiple purposes that traditional units do not. Not only do we connect people who host storage to renters, we also connect renters to other renters who want to split a storage space in the case that they have unused space.

How do I find storage?

You can search for listings based on location, price, space, and available dates. See more on the how it works page!

Are my belongings safe?

We have user verification via student emails to ensure outsiders can not access your personal belongings. We also have insurance measures in place such that if any of your belongings are damaged or missing, we will issue you a full refund of how much you paid for storage.

For Hosts:

How do I list my space?

You can create a listing by providing location, type of space (room/closet/garage, etc.), size estimate, price, and available dates.

How do I decide pricing?

We have AI algorithms that detect the current market trends for storage space to help guide you in setting your price.
