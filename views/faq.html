About

StudentStorage was founded in 2026 as a student-driven initiative to address the seasonal challenges of campus move-outs and short-term storage gaps. Developed in response to the lack of affordable and flexible options available to students, the platform brings together peers across campuses to better utilize existing space. By building on shared student experiences, StudentStorage aims to create a more coordinated and accessible approach to summer storage.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is StudentStorage?

Student storage is a platform that connects peers who need short-term storage with other students who either have the available storage they need or also need storage (for the purposes of sharing one storage space together).

How is this different from traditional storage units?

StudentStorage is a far more affordable option than traditional storage units. We don’t own any actual storage units, but rather we connect students who need storage to students that have storage.
Furthermore, this system is designed to serve multiple purposes that traditional units do not. Not only do we connect people who host storage to renters, we also connect renters to other renters who want to split a storage space in the case that they have unused space.

Are my belongings safe?

We have user verification via student emails to ensure outsiders can not access your personal belongings. We also have insurance measures in place such that if any of your belongings are damaged or missing, we will issue you a full refund of how much you paid for storage.

Can I access my items at any time?

That depends on the storage provider.
